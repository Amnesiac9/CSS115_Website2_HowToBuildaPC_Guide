Journey to Mars

History

  • The first rover to successfully land on Mars and communicate back to earth was the Sojourner rover, which was launched on December 4, 1996 and landed on Mars on July 4, 1997. It was the first rover to land on Mars and was the first rover to be controlled remotely from Earth. It was also the first rover to take a selfie on Mars.

    • Mars Rover Sojourner, taken by Pathfinder in 1997.

  • Exploration is continued to this day by two other rovers, Curiosity and Perseverance. Curiosity was launched on November 26, 2011 and landed on Mars on August 6, 2012. It is still operational today. Perseverance was launched on July 30, 2020 and landed on Mars on February 18, 2021. It is also still operational today.
